PM-KISAN (PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMMAN NIDHI)

PM-KISAN provides direct income support of Rs 6,000 per year to small and marginal farmers.
The amount is paid in three equal installments of Rs 2,000 every four months directly to the bank accounts of eligible farmers.

Eligibility Criteria:
- All land-holding farmers with cultivable land
- Both small (less than 2 hectares) and large farmers are eligible
- Farmer must have valid Aadhaar card linked to bank account

Exclusions:
- Institutional land holders
- Government employees and pensioners
- Income tax payers
- Former and present MPs/MLAs/Ministers

How to Register:
1. Visit nearest Common Service Centre
2. Submit Aadhaar, land records, and bank details
3. Alternatively register at pmkisan.gov.in

SOIL HEALTH CARD SCHEME

The Soil Health Card Scheme provides soil health cards to farmers with crop-wise nutrient recommendations.
Cards are provided free of cost and updated every two years.

Benefits:
- Reduces fertilizer expenditure by 10-20%
- Improves crop yield by 5-6%
- Helps in making right decisions about fertilizer application
- Reduces soil degradation

KISAN CREDIT CARD (KCC) SCHEME

Kisan Credit Card provides short-term credit to farmers for agricultural operations.
Credit limit based on land holding and cropping pattern.
Interest rate as low as 4% per annum with government subsidy.
Loan up to Rs 3 lakh at 7% interest with 3% interest subvention for timely repayment.

Eligibility: All farmers, tenant farmers, oral lessees, sharecroppers, SHG or Joint Liability Groups.
Documents: Aadhaar, land records, recent photograph.
