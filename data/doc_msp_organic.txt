MINIMUM SUPPORT PRICE (MSP) FOR CROPS 2024-25

The government provides MSP as guaranteed price for agricultural produce to protect farmers from distress selling.

Current MSP (2024-25):
- Paddy (Common): Rs 2,300 per quintal
- Wheat: Rs 2,275 per quintal
- Jowar (Hybrid): Rs 3,371 per quintal
- Bajra: Rs 2,625 per quintal
- Maize: Rs 2,090 per quintal
- Moong: Rs 8,682 per quintal
- Urad: Rs 7,400 per quintal
- Arhar/Tur: Rs 7,550 per quintal
- Groundnut: Rs 6,783 per quintal
- Sunflower: Rs 7,280 per quintal
- Soybean: Rs 4,892 per quintal
- Sesame: Rs 9,267 per quintal
- Cotton (Long): Rs 7,521 per quintal
- Mustard: Rs 5,950 per quintal
- Ragi: Rs 4,290 per quintal

How to Sell at MSP:
- Register on the state government procurement portal
- Bring produce to nearest procurement centre
- Government agencies like FCI, NAFED procure at MSP

PARAMPARAGAT KRISHI VIKAS YOJANA (PKVY) - ORGANIC FARMING

Financial assistance up to Rs 50,000 per hectare over 3 years for organic farming conversion.
This includes Rs 31,000 for organic inputs, Rs 8,800 for value addition and marketing, Rs 2,000 for certification.

Benefits:
- Premium price for organic produce (20-30% higher)
- Reduced input costs
- Better soil health
- Access to premium markets

Eligibility: Farmers willing to convert to organic farming with group formation of minimum 20 farmers.

RASHTRIYA KRISHI VIKAS YOJANA (RKVY)

Infrastructure development grants for:
- Farm machinery purchase: up to 50% subsidy
- Irrigation equipment: up to 55% subsidy for SC/ST farmers, 45% for general category
- Cold storage construction: up to 35% capital subsidy
- Poly house/greenhouse: up to 50% subsidy for small farmers

MICRO IRRIGATION FUND

National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) manages a Rs 5,000 crore corpus.
Subsidies available for drip and sprinkler irrigation systems:
- Small/Marginal farmers: 55% subsidy
- Other farmers: 45% subsidy
- SC/ST farmers: 60% subsidy

Contact your district agriculture office for application forms and assistance.
